## Websocket Flow ################################
 Host_Bot (Server): 
    -MANGAES GAME STATES AND ROUTES MESSAGES BETWEEN CLIENT BOTS and user.
    -Each client bot connects to a server bot.
    -THE ARCADE BOT HANDLES sending updates, MEMBER COMMANDS, or 
        actions TO SPECIFIC CLIENTS.

 Game_Bots (Clients): 
    -They handle their specialized logic but rely on the server bot for 
        session management and communication between memebers.

## Websocket Server ################################
 Host_Bot
    -Coordinates games.
    -Manages player displays and invites.
    -Relays messages between game bots and players.

## Websocket Client ################################
 Game_Bot
    -Handle individual game (libraries) logic (e.g., Guess My Number, Battleship, [board games]).
    -Respond to commands or requests sent from the arcade bot.
    -Communicate game results or updates back to the arcade bot.
